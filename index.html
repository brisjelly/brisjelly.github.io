Bris Jelly

Fridays from 12pm (arrive anytime) / @The Edge

What is Jelly?

It’s a casual meetup anyone can join, but we’re mostly remote workers who could do with a bit of socialization at the end of the week.

We meet at The Edge to have a chat while working, doodling or pondering the science of potatoes.

When & Where

At The Edge — part of the State Library of Queensland in South Brisbane.

It’s every Friday.
Arrive anytime from 12 noon, but there are usually more people there after ~2pm.

RSVP?

Nah, it’s a bit casual for that. Just turn up if you’re keen. No weapons please.

Jelly on Slack

Bris Jelly has a channel on the Brisbane Slack. Hop on for a chat or to get in touch with us.
