Bris Jelly

What is Jelly?

Jelly was historically a casual meetup anyone can join, mostly remote workers who could do with a bit of socialization at the end of the week.

The in-person meetups don't happen regularly anymore, but we're still active on Slack, and there's occassionally a meet up or casual drinks that might come out of that.

When & Where

Formally we met at The Edge — part of the State Library of Queensland in South Brisbane.

This is still a great place to book a window booth or get a table with river views and hang out or do some work.
